Help Tom escape!

Control the bridges to help him escape the overgrown Earth Bees! BBZZZZZZ

Controls:

Press SPACE to operate the bridges. It’s simply a matter of timing. You are the only thing that stands between Tom and him being stung to death by two overgrown Earth Bees!

Game elements

Bridge:

Extendible, and more importantly, sturdy. Tom loves them.

 

Information

  • This was created during Ludum Dare 21, where you program a game from scratch, within 48 hours. I started late. The theme for Ludum Date #21 was “Escape.”
  • 65-73-63 is “esc” in hex. : )
  • I planned to put in launchers, boosters, and doors. I just did not have the time. I’ll probably do that later on.
  • The level is really short. I was going to try randomly generate the level, but I didn’t get around to that either. Maybe in an update.
  • My goal for Ludum Dare 21 was to get some familiarity with CoffeeScript, as I’ll be using it when Rails 3.1 is released. I feel I did that, and I love it.
